# Design Rationale – Conditioned Hairdressing Website Redesign

## Introduction
This project focuses on redesigning the website of Conditioned Hairdressing, a professional hair salon located in Midrand, South Africa. The goal of the redesign was to improve the user experience and increase the likelihood that visitors will book appointments or contact the salon.

Hair salon websites are often used as a primary way for customers to discover services, view styling options, and book appointments. Therefore, the design must be visually appealing, easy to navigate, and focused on guiding users toward booking a service.

## Target Users
The main users of the website include:

• Individuals looking for professional haircuts and styling  
• Clients seeking hair colour and treatments  
• Customers searching for premium haircare salons  
• Existing customers who want to book appointments online  

## Problem with Many Salon Websites
Many salon websites struggle with:

• Unclear service descriptions  
• Poor navigation and layout  
• Lack of clear booking options  
• Weak calls-to-action  

These issues can cause visitors to leave the website without contacting the business.

## UX Design Decisions

### 1. Clear Homepage Messaging
The redesigned homepage immediately explains what the salon offers and highlights its premium haircare services.

### 2. Strong Call-To-Action Buttons
Call-to-action buttons such as “Book Appointment” and “Contact Us” are placed strategically across the website to guide users toward making a booking.

### 3. Service Section Organization
Services such as haircuts, colouring, and treatments are grouped into clearly structured sections to help users quickly understand what the salon offers.

### 4. Visual Hierarchy
A clean layout with clear headings, spacing, and section separation was used to improve readability and guide user attention.

### 5. Brand and Product Emphasis
Premium haircare brands such as L’Oréal, Kérastase and Kevin Murphy are highlighted to build trust and reflect the salon’s high-quality services.

### 6. Mobile Responsiveness
The design ensures that the website works well on both desktop and mobile devices since many users access salon websites from smartphones.

## Conversion Strategy
The redesign focuses on increasing bookings by:

• Using prominent booking buttons
• Simplifying the contact section
• Providing clear service descriptions
• Displaying trust elements such as testimonials and product brands

These elements help reduce user friction and encourage visitors to take action.

## Conclusion
The redesigned website provides a modern, clean, and user-focused experience that better represents the quality and professionalism of the salon. By improving navigation, messaging, and calls-to-action, the redesign aims to increase engagement and encourage more clients to book salon services.
